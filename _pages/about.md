I'm currently a first year computer science PhD student at the University of Waterloo, working with <a href="https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~ppoupart/">Prof. Pascal Poupart</a>. My main interests are around NLP and probabilistic machine learning.

Previously I completed my CS master's also at the University of Waterloo with Prof. Pascal Poupart. My thesis work focused on using grammatical error detection for in-context example selection for grammatical error correction using LLMs.

I received my bachelor's in computer science from the University of São Paulo in 2020. During my time as an undergraduate I've co-founded <a href="http://data.icmc.usp.br/">Data</a>, a student group focused on machine learning related topics. I've also worked at Samsung R&D Institute Brazil, doing research on deep learning for computer vision.
